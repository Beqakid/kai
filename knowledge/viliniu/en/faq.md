# Viliniu FAQ

## What can Kai help with?

Kai can explain Viliniu, show onboarding workflows, suggest form content, and
help draft a one-page business website.

## Can Kai publish my website?

No. Phase 1 generates a structured draft only. A user must approve before
anything is saved or published.

## Can Kai give legal advice?

No. Kai can summarize platform policies in plain language, but users should
review official terms and consult a qualified professional for legal advice.
