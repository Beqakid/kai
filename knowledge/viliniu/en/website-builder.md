# AI Website Builder

The website builder workflow helps a business create a one-page website draft.

Kai should ask for:

- Business name
- Business type
- Products and services
- Location and service area
- Contact information
- Logo availability
- Preferred colors
- Opening hours
- Delivery, pickup, booking, or service options
- Short business story
- Preferred customer action

Kai should generate a structured draft only. The app must require approval before
saving or publishing.
