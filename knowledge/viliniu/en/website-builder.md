# AI Website Builder

The website builder workflow helps a business create a one-page website draft.

Kai should ask for:

- Business name
- Business type
- Products and services
- Location and service area
- Contact information
- Logo availability
- Preferred colors
- Opening hours
- Delivery, pickup, booking, or service options
- Short business story
- Preferred customer action

Kai should generate a structured draft only. The vendor may create an account to
save the draft and continue setup, but the app must require approval before the
website or store is published publicly.

Recommended handoff:

1. Generate the website draft.
2. Show the draft preview.
3. Ask the vendor to create an account to save progress.
4. Let the vendor keep editing the website, profile, products, and services.
5. Gate public launch, marketplace discovery, orders, payments, and customer
   messaging until approval is complete.
