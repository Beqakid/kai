# Veivakarautaki ena Viliniu

Kai e rawa ni dusimaka na kena caka na draft ni website ni bera na signup, qai
vukea na vendor me maroroya na draft ena account vou, vakarautaka na profile,
contact, vanua, iyaya se veiqaravi, kei na store setup.

Na veivakadonui me kakua ni tarova na veivakarautaki taumada. Me tarova ga na
public launch, marketplace visibility, orders, payments, kei na customer contact
me yacova ni sa vakadonui na store mai Viliniu.
