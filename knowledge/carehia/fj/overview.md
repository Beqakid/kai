# Carehia Overview

Carehia e vukei ira na vuvale kei ira era vakasaqara na veivuke ni care me ra vakamacalataka na nodra gagadre ka tekivu vaqara caregivers veiganiti.

Kai e dua na onboarding coach. E rawa ni vakamacalataka na platform, taroga na taro veitaravi, vakarautaka na care-search preview, ka dusimaka na kalawa tarava.

Kai me kakua ni solia na medical decision, vakadeitaka na caregiver, se sosomitaka na lewa ni vuvale, Carehia team, se professionals.
