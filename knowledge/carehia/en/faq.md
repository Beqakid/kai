# Carehia FAQ

## What can Kai help with?

Kai can explain Carehia, guide care-search onboarding, collect care needs, and prepare a summary for the next step.

## Does Kai choose the caregiver?

No. Kai helps prepare the search. Caregiver selection, verification, approval, and final decisions stay with Carehia's process and the family.

## Can Kai save my care search?

In the demo, Kai only prepares a preview. Production saving should require user approval and follow Carehia's privacy process.
